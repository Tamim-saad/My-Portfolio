Hello! I am Tamim Hasan Saad from Dhaka, Bangladesh. I am currently a 4th-year CSE student at BUET.

My hometown is Pabna, but I am currently residing in Mirpur, Dhaka. I completed my JSC and SSC from Uttara High School and my HSC from Rajuk Uttara Model College.

I have a passion for problem-solving and web development. I am deeply interested in learning new technologies and contributing to society. I have already learned C, C++, Java, Python HTML, CSS, and SQL for database management. I try to participate in coding challenges on platforms like Codeforces and CSES, and I use GitHub regularly to share my projects.

I am thrilled to have you visit my portfolio. Feel free to explore my website, and don't hesitate to send me any queries through the contact page.

School

I attended Uttara High School and College, located at Uttara.

I completed my PSC, JSC, and SSC exams here, achieving a GPA of 5.00 in each. I was the first boy among over 300 students in the day shift from class 8 to class 10 for three consecutive years.

I am profoundly grateful to my school teachers, who laid a strong foundation for my education. I am especially thankful to Mr. Shahnoor Showkat, my math, physics, and chemistry teacher, for his exceptional guidance in building my foundational knowledge in these subjects.

Additionally, I made some kind-hearted friends during my school years and cherished every moment spent there. I have fond memories of my time at school and appreciate the positive impact it had on my life.

College

After completing my SSC exam, I enrolled at Rajuk Uttara Model College for my higher secondary education.

This was the best period of my student life. I met extraordinary teachers and classmates who significantly influenced my academic journey. My respected teachers were always supportive, and my classmates were exceptionally talented. The overall environment motivated me to study diligently and consistently.

I managed to secure a position within the top 20 in each internal exam at the college. Ultimately, I completed my HSC with a GPA of 5.00.

The combination of my dedicated teachers, kind-hearted classmates, and the superb environment created some of the best memories of my student life.
